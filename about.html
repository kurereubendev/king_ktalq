About King Ktalq


R&B singer-songwriter King Ktalq, hailing from the vibrant music scene of Kaduna, Nigeria, is captivating audiences with his soulful melodies and heartfelt lyrics. King Ktalq's music is a tapestry of raw emotion, genuine expression, and a unique sound that is sure to resonate with listeners worldwide.

King Ktalq is a passionate singer and songwriter hailing from Kaduna, Nigeria. With a soulful voice and captivating lyrics, he has made a mark in the music industry, creating a unique blend of R&B that resonates with audiences worldwide.

Born and raised in Kano, King Ktalq discovered his love for music at an early age. Influenced by a diverse range of artists, his sound is a fusion of "Good Music". His music not only reflects his personal experiences but also touches on universal themes, creating a connection with listeners on a profound level.

King Ktalq has graced the stage at numerous venues and events, delivering powerful performances that leave a lasting impression. His ability to convey raw emotions through his music has garnered him a dedicated fan base, and his songs have been featured on various platforms, earning critical acclaim.

King Ktalq creates music that inspires and uplifts. Join the journey and be a part of the movement.
